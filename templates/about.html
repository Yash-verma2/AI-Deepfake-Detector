About Our Deepfake Detection AI

In an age where artificial intelligence can mimic human voices with near-perfect precision, it’s more important than ever to protect truth and authenticity. Our Voice Deepfake Detector uses state-of-the-art machine learning models to distinguish between genuine human speech and AI-generated fakes.

🎯 Purpose

To combat misinformation, protect digital identity, and bring awareness to the growing capabilities of generative AI technologies.

🧠 Technology

Powered by machine learning and audio signal processing, our system analyzes voice patterns using MFCCs (Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficients) and neural networks trained on real and synthetic voice datasets.

🚀 Accuracy

Our model achieves high confidence scores, visualized through interactive UI elements like circular meters and real-time feedback for a smooth and professional user experience.

🌐 Vision

This is just the beginning. Soon, we’ll launch our Image Deepfake Detector and expand into other AI abuse domains—ensuring safety, transparency, and trust across all digital content.
